Help and FAQ

Q: What is linerub?

Linerub is a new and creative way to interact with friends. Nowadays users have complete control over their posts. At Linerub, we encourage collaboration: one user writes a post, other users tag that post with pictures. It's simple, interactive, and legal.


Q: Who came up with the name linerub?

A: We did.


Q: How do I create a story?

A: It’s simple! Expand the sidebar by clicking on the top-right button, and then click “Share a story”. You can create a post in 2 simple steps!


Q: How do other users add pictures to my story?

A: In Step 2 you can add tags by selecting words. These words can be tagged with pictures by anyone that views your post.


Q: How do I view pictures on other posts?

A: Click on a post. The post box will expand and all uploaded pictures will appear. View pictures by clicking on highlighted words.


Q: How do I add pictures to other posts?

A: After the post expands, click on the “add pics!” button on the top right corner of the post box. Select any of the highlighted words and follow the instructions.


Q: Where can I view my published posts and drafts?

A: Click on the “My stories” tab in the sidebar.


Q: Where can I view my profile?

A: Click on the “View my profile” tab in the sidebar.


Q: What does the “exchange!” button do?

A: Glad that you asked! By clicking on “exchange!”, you will get a random story from a random Lineruber! You can “like” the post and follow the user.


Q: Can I edit or delete my published posts?

A: Of course you have the freedom to delete your own post! Unfortunately, you can only edit the tags that you selected. So...edit well in your draft! ;)
