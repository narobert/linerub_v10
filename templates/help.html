Help and FAQ

Q: What is Linerub?

Linerub is a new and creative way to interact with friends. Nowadays users have complete control over their posts. At Linerub, we encourage collaboration: one user writes a post, other users tag that post with pictures. It's simple, interactive, and free.


Q: Who came up with the name "Linerub"?

A: We did.


Q: How do I create a post?

A: It’s simple! Expand the sidebar by clicking on the top-right button, and then click “Share a story”. You can create a post in 2 simple steps!


Q: When can I add pictures to my post?

A: In Step 2 you select words in your story. These words become tags that can later be tagged with pictures by anyone that views your post, including you!


Q: How do I view pictures on other posts?

A: Click on a post and the post box will expand. View pictures by clicking on highlighted words.


Q: How do I add pictures to other posts?

A: After the post expands, click on the “add pics” button. Select any of the highlighted words and follow the instructions.


Q: Where can I view my published posts and drafts?

A: Click on the “My stories” tab in the sidebar.


Q: What does the “Get random!” button do?

A: Glad that you asked! By clicking on "Get random!”, you will get a random story from a random Lineruber! You can “like” the post and follow the user.


Q: Can I edit or delete my published posts?

A: Of course you have the freedom to delete your own post! Unfortunately, you can only edit posts that haven't been tagged with images. These posts will be under "Saved Drafts".
